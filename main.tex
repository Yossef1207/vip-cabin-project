\chapter*{Abstract}
This report consolidates the literature and design logic most relevant to the InnovAero task of improving acoustic comfort in a VIP aircraft cabin. The focus is on methods that are technically defensible and implementation-oriented: active noise control, adaptive filtering, virtual sensing, cabin-specific acoustic constraints, and hybrid passive-active concepts. The resulting structure is intended to support an academically rigorous submission that can be extended with competition-specific calculations, design assumptions, and verification data.
