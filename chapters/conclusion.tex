\chapter{Conclusion and Outlook}

\section{Conclusion}
The literature indicates that no single technique is sufficient for a premium aircraft cabin. Passive measures are robust, active control is effective in the low-frequency range, and hybrid systems provide the best balance between performance and feasibility. For an InnovAero submission, the strongest position is therefore a localized hybrid concept supported by a transparent methodology.

\section{Outlook}
The next development step should be a more detailed concept study with explicit geometry, control-zone definition, and disturbance assumptions. After that, the report can be expanded with simulation results, a comparison of candidate architectures, and a refined implementation discussion for Lufthansa Technik review.
